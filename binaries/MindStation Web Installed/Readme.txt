B) INSTALL/UNINSTALL TIPS 
 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
WINDOWS(r) 95/98/ME/XP and WINDOWS(r) 2000/NT 4 with Service Pack 5+ INSTALLING
 
Insert the LeapFrog Mind Station CD into your CD-ROM drive. Autorun will automatically install Mind Station and LeapPrint software. Follow the onscreen instructions.

For systems with autorun disabled, from the START MENU, choose RUN. 
Type d:\autostart in the Command Line box, then click OK. (In this example 'd' is the CD drive.) 
Follow the onscreen instructions.


WINDOWS(r) 95/98/ME/XP and WINDOWS(r) 2000/NT 4 with Service Pack 5+ UNINSTALLING


1) You can uninstall Mind Station and LeapPrint software through the ADD/REMOVE PROGRAMS feature. To do this, you should go to START, SETTINGS, CONTROL PANEL and then double click on the ADD/REMOVE PROGRAMS icon. From the Install/Uninstall tab, scroll down until you find LeapFrog Mind Station. Highlight the program to uninstall and click the 'ADD/REMOVE' button. 
 
2) You can select 'uninstall' from your START/PROGRAM/LeapFrog menu. 
 

C) GENERAL TROUBLESHOOTING TIPS
 
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 
If you experience any problems during a download or upload between the Mind Station connector and the LeapFrog Web Site, there are several possible solutions:
 
a) Restart the Mind Station by removing and reinserting the cartridge. Ensure that you have power - the AC adapter is preferred for best power. A green light should indicate that your connector is operating properly. Then reattempt download or upload.
   
b) Your phone line might be experiencing problems. Make sure that your phone line is not in use by other members of your family. If you experience problems with your internet connection - contact your Internet Service Provider.
  
c) Check all cable connections: Mind Station USB is preferred for Windows 98/ME/XP/2000, or serial cable to your PC 9-pin serial port required for Windows 95 and NT. 
   
d) Slow-downs can occur on the Internet at peak periods of time. If you experience a slow-down, try again at another time. If the problem persists, or if it only happens on the LeapFrog site, contact our support group at www.leapfrog.com/support.

e) Check with your Internet Service Provider if you have any problem connecting to the Internet, getting a modem connection when you have a dial tone, or have any other problems with regard to the Internet.
 
To view LeapPrint troubleshooting tips, launch the program and click the Troubleshoot button in the toolbar.
